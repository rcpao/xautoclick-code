
<B>CVS</B>
<P>

You can get the latest development sources from CVS.
<P>
<HR>

<B>Users</B>
<P>
Checking out the source code:
<P>
<DIV CLASS="commandline"><PRE><B STYLE="color: black;">cvs -d:pserver:anonymous@cvs.sourceforge.net:/cvsroot/xautoclick login
cvs -z3 -d:pserver:anonymous@cvs.sourceforge.net:/cvsroot/xautoclick co -P xautoclick

</B></PRE>
</DIV>
<P>
Updating an existing checkout:
<P>
<DIV CLASS="commandline"><PRE><B STYLE="color: black;">cvs -z3 -d:pserver:anonymous@cvs.sourceforge.net:/cvsroot/xautoclick up -dPA

</B></PRE>
</DIV>

<HR>

<B>Developers</B>
<P>
<DIV CLASS="commandline"><PRE><B STYLE="color: black;">export CVS_RSH=ssh
cvs -z3 -d:ext:developername@cvs.sourceforge.net:/cvsroot/xautoclick co -P xautoclick

</B></PRE>
</DIV>
