
<B>Subversion</B>
<P>

You can get the latest development sources from Subversion.
<P>
<HR>

<B>Users</B>
<P>
Checking out the source code:
<P>
<DIV CLASS="commandline"><PRE><B STYLE="color: black;">svn co https://svn.sourceforge.net/svnroot/xautoclick/xautoclick

</B></PRE>
</DIV>
<P>
Updating an existing checkout:
<P>
<DIV CLASS="commandline"><PRE><B STYLE="color: black;">svn up

</B></PRE>
</DIV>

<HR>

<B>Developers</B>
<P>
<DIV CLASS="commandline"><PRE><B STYLE="color: black;">svn co https://svn.sourceforge.net/svnroot/xautoclick xautoclick
svn ci &lt;file&gt; --username &lt;username&gt;

</B></PRE>
</DIV>
